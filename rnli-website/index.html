About the RNLI

The Royal National Lifeboat Institution (RNLI) is a charity that saves lives at sea. It provides a 24-hour search and rescue service across the UK, Ireland, Isle of Man, and Channel Islands.

With over 238 lifeboat stations and thousands of volunteer crew members, the RNLI responds to over 8,000 call-outs every year — entirely funded by donations.
